                <li><strong>Database:</strong> MySQL</li>
                <li><strong>Framework:</strong> Laravel</li>
            </ul>
        </section>

        <section>
            <h2>Features</h2>
            <ul>
                <li><strong>Login & Authentication:</strong> Includes secure login, password reset, email verification, and Google Sign-In functionality.</li>
                <li><strong>User Dashboard:</strong> Allows users to manage their certificates, work experience, and educational background, as well as schedule counseling appointments and upload their resumes.</li>
                <li><strong>Employer Dashboard:</strong> Enables employers to manage job listings and directly review job applications.</li>
                <li><strong>Admin Dashboard:</strong> Provides administrators with a comprehensive view of the entire platform, allowing them to monitor and manage various aspects of the system.</li>
            </ul>
        </section>

        <section>
            <h2>Weaknesses</h2>
            <ul>
                <li><strong>Static User Interface:</strong> The current design is functional but lacks dynamic elements and modern user experience enhancements.</li>
                <li><strong>Performance Issues:</strong> The website may experience performance bottlenecks and slow loading times during high traffic due to unoptimized queries and database operations.</li>
                <li><strong>Scalability Challenges:</strong> The existing architecture could face difficulties handling a high volume of concurrent users efficiently.</li>
